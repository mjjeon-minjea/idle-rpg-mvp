## Equipment Architecture Decisions (장비 아키텍처 결정사항)

```text
PlayerState = 성장으로 오른 기본 스탯 원본
EquipmentState = 장착한 itemId 목록
EffectivePlayerStats = 전투에 사용하는 최종 스탯
```

Important rules:

- Equipping does not reduce inventory quantity in MVP 1.
- Equip requires `InventorySystem.hasItem(itemId, 1) === true`.
- Equipment stats must not be directly accumulated into `player.attack`, `player.defense`, or `player.maxHp`.
- Equipment bonus is calculated every time through `EquipmentSystem.calculateEffectiveStats(player)`.
- Equip does not heal the player.
- Unequip clamps `player.hp` down to effective max HP if needed.
- DropResolver (드랍 계산기) and RewardResolver (보상 계산기) stay unchanged in responsibility.
